Open Source License Introduction and Analysis


  • Introduction and Customization

    • Introduction

      Introduce most of the commonly used, mainstream licenses, including providing detailed text of the terms and conditions, and generalizing the terms and conditions for easy reading.


    • Customization

      Present the user with most of the terms of the license and make preliminary judgments based on the customer's requirements for these terms and filter out the appropriate licenses for the customer.

  • Compliance and Conflict Analysis

    • Compliance Analysis

      Find known license information in the user-uploaded folder, including the license name and version number, and display the results on the screen.


    • Conflict Analysis

      Perform a compatibility analysis of all licenses in the user-uploaded folder and give the final result. The end result may be a license for the entire project, or it may be conflicting information and modifications between licenses.
